Email us a description or a markup of the changes you need to produce your shop drawings. We can provide a quote for your job.

02. Mid-scale

Have a low rise or medium rise building work and looking for engineering or drafting service?

Not quite ready to engage full-time staff but require professional help for your project?
We can sit down with you to go over your job scope and devise a part-time arrangement to work on your building services design and shop drawings.

03. Large-scale

Growing your design team to work on building design and construct projects?

Engage a dedicated building professional in a hybrid outsource structure.
We recruit new talents, on-shore or off-shore, and assign them to work on your projects full-time.
The recruitment will be tailored to your unique requirements and you will be invited to interview handpicked candidates.
Let us handle the workstation, BIM 360 setup and payroll so that you can focus on meeting builders' construction programs!

Professional Engineers Registration

The Professional Engineers Registration Act 2019 requires one in Victoria to be registered with the Business Licensing Authority (BLA) to provide professional engineering services. The registration allows BIM Farers to issue Regulation 126 certificate of compliance for building permits and offer engineered building service designs via Performance Solutions.
